Ben and Elizabeth met at Parkside church and fell in love. Ben asked Elizabeth to marry her one fall evening on their one year anniversary after a walk in the park. Elizabeth never saw it coming and she squealed in excitement and gave a resounding yes as Ben asked her to spend the rest of their lives together. Emerging from a cave were excited friends who got to share in such a special moment with them before they went off to dinner. After the wedding they plan on moving in to their new house in Middlefield where they will start the next chapter of their lives as husband and wife.
